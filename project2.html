CHARM

overview

Despite having a strong brand identity, compelling narrative, and a clear value proposition centered on health-conscious social confidence, Charm—a student-founded non-alcoholic beverage brand—lacks a dedicated website. This absence significantly limits the brand’s ability to connect with its target audience of socially active, wellness-focused young people. Without a digital presence, Charm cannot effectively communicate its mission, showcase its products, or engage customers who rely on online platforms for discovery, trust-building, and purchase decisions.
