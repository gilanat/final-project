Caydence Records & Coffee

overview

A homepage website redesign for Caydence Records & Coffee, a unique local space combining a record store and coffee shop. This project focused on improving user experience across both desktop and mobile by incorporating ideation, wireframing, and prototyping. The goal was to better reflect the store's eclectic identity, streamline navigation, and enhance access to menus, events, and music releases.
